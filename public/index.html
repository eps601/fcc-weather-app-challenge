This application used the server location of your internet service provider for weather in your area.

For a more precise location (or for any location world-wide), enter any of the following.
  • City Name e.g. Augusta defaults to Augusta, Italy but Augusta, Georgia or Augusta, Western Australia will get weather for those locations;
  • UK postcode;
  • Canada postal code;
  • US zip code
The wind vane:
  • shaft points from where the wind is coming
  • flag indicates winds around 50 knots
  • long barb indicates winds around 10 knots
  • short barb indicates winds around 5 knots
  • no barb winds < 3 knots (just the cloud circle)
Cloud cover
  • no shading <15%
  • one quarter shading >15%, <35%
  • half shading >35%, <65%
  • three quarters >65%, <85%
  • circle fully shaded >85%
Example: Just a circle indicates no clouds and calm winds.
